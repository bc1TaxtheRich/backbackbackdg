The license

Buying SeoBooster gets you a one-time, non-exclusive license to install and run the desktop app on machines you control, and to connect it to sites you own or are authorized to audit. It is not a subscription — there's no recurring charge.

The trial

New installs include a 7-day free trial with full feature access, no card required. If you don't purchase a license, the trial simply ends and scanning stops — nothing is charged automatically.

Acceptable use

  • Only connect SeoBooster to sites you own or have permission to crawl and audit.
  • Don't use SeoBooster to overload, disrupt, or attack a site — it's a diagnostic tool, not a load-testing tool.
  • Don't redistribute, resell, or share your license key outside your own organization.

Updates

Your license includes free updates for 1 year from purchase. After that, the version you have keeps working — renewing gets you further updates.

No warranty on SEO outcomes

SeoBooster reports on what it finds — broken links, backlink changes, structural issues. Fixing what it flags is generally good practice, but we can't guarantee specific ranking or traffic outcomes, since those also depend on factors outside the app.

Refunds

Because there's a full-featured 7-day trial before purchase, license purchases are final. If something is broken on our end, contact support and we'll make it right.

Changes to these terms

We may update these terms as the product changes. Material changes will be noted on this page with an updated date.
