Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

What SeoBooster is

SeoBooster is a desktop application for Windows. This policy covers the marketing site you're reading now and the desktop app itself.

What runs locally

When you connect a site inside the app, the crawl, the link checks, and the AI audit all run from your own machine. Reports are written to your local disk. We don't require a dashboard login, and we don't proxy your site's traffic through our servers to perform a scan.

What we collect

  • License activation: your email address and license key, so the app can verify a valid trial or purchase.
  • Update checks: the installed version number, so the app knows when a newer build is available.
  • Contact form submissions: whatever you enter on the Contact page, so we can reply to you.

We don't collect the contents of the sites you scan, the URLs you audit, or the reports SeoBooster generates.

Payment information

License purchases are processed by a third-party payment provider. SeoBooster does not store your card details.

Sharing

We don't sell personal data. Information is shared only with the service providers needed to run license activation, payments, and email delivery, and only as far as required to provide those services.

Your choices

You can request a copy of the data we hold on your license, or ask us to delete it, by writing to privacy@seobooster.dev.
